Opportunity Keyword Search

  • Type the opportunity keyword in the text field.

  • Click the Search button or press enter button.

  • You can see a progress bar and then you can see the list of opportunities.

  • From the opportunity list you can click on the eye icon to view to opportunity, and its corresponding outlet and contacts

  • You can add this opportunity as your opportunity and you can view it later.

  • You can also add reminder or notes to this opportunity for future reference.
